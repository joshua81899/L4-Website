OurHistroy

In the early 1950s, Tom and Mary, a young couple, shared a love of camping and the outdoors. They frequently found it difficult to obtain quality camping equipment, so they decided to open their own camping equipment store, appropriately named "Retail Camping Company." Their store immediately became a popular location for local outdoor lovers. Tom and Mary took care in carrying only the best gear, from high-quality tents to tough backpacks and sleeping bags. Their company flourished over time, and they expanded their product offerings to include hiking and climbing equipment. They also started offering camping trips and outdoor courses, where they could share their knowledge and passion for nature with their consumers. Tom and Mary still own and run Retail Camping Company today. The store has become a community institution, noted for its courteous service and high-quality merchandise. Generations of campers continue to rely on the pair for all of their outdoor requirements, carrying on the couple's tradition.
